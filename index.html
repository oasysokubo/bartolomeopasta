We are the Facciolla Brothers

Bartolomeo Facciolla was raised at family farm in South Italy, surrounded in Olive trees, vineyard, tomatoes, all organic farms operated by family.

Bartolomeo and his 2 brothers moved to USA and they started to import family's great treasure - extra virgin olive oils to share the best products directly from South Italy. Our fresh olive oils are very special and you must try to taste different from ones selling at stores.

We are very proud of sharing our family's treasure with people in US.
